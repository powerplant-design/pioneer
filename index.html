Pioneer Specialise In

Soakage Pits and Sub Soil Drainage

Need effective surface water management for your property?

We specialise in soakage pits and sub-soil drainage solutions to prevent flooding, control groundwater, and protect your foundations.

Whether you're dealing with excess water pooling or poor drainage, we ensure a durable and effective system tailored to your needs.

Stormwater or Sewer Drainage

Ensure your section is free draining with our expert stormwater drainage services.

We install, repair, and maintain drainage systems, including channel drains and sumps, to prevent surface water buildup and manage stormwater effectively.

We also handle domestic sewerage, from repair and maintenance of exisiting systems, through to installing new pre-slab drains and pipework.

LIFESTYLE BLOCK SOLUTIONS, PROPERTY WATER SUPPLIES AND DRAINAGE

Need your lifestyle block field drains cleared out or a piped culvert drain crossing installed? Struggling to hold on to that precious rainwater? Or is your main water supply pipework leaking?

We install and repair property water supplies and drainage, fixing or renewing pipework, tanks or pumps. Providing tailored solutions for rural and urban properties, be it the water piped in from the councils toby at the street or your rural rain water tanks and overflow.

Hot Water Systems & Gas Leak Repairs

Reliable hot water solutions and leak repairs. We install and service hot water systems to keep your home running efficiently.

If you suspect a gas leak, we provide prompt and professional tests, checks and repairs to ensure safety and prevent damage.
